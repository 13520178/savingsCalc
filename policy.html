Information Collection and Use

The Application performs savings and investment calculations directly on your device. Information you enter (such as savings amount, interest rate, contribution amount, or time period) is processed locally on your device.

No personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or contact information) is collected by the Application.

The Application does not upload your financial calculation data to any server.

In some cases, the Application may collect limited non-personal technical information such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version)
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash reports

This information is collected anonymously to improve the performance and stability of the Application.

Some illustrations, icons, or language refinements used in the Application may be generated or assisted by artificial intelligence technologies during the content creation process.

AI tools are used only to assist with design, illustration, or translation. They do not provide financial advice.

The Application is intended as a financial calculator for educational and informational purposes only.
